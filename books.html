Book Collection

This project is more of a personal goal for me. In here, I will list most (hopefully all) of the books I have been reading throughout my life. The purpose of it is to help me keep track of the main ideas or fascinating quotes I have found in the literary works I've read. Hopefully this will serve for me as inspiration and aid for future projects. Similarly it can be useful for people in case you are wondering in a good book to read!
